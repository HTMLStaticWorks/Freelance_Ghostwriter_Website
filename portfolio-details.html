Portfolio Detail

The Invisible Architect

By David Chen | Genre: Business & Leadership

Project Summary

A deep dive into the organizational structures and psychological frameworks of high-performance tech teams. The book covers leadership, cultural engineering, and scaling innovation in hyper-competitive markets. Ghostwritten over a 10-month period through intensive weekly interviews with David Chen, CEO of Arch-Tech Solutions.

The Writing Process

The project started with a comprehensive "Voice Audit" where I studied David's previous articles, speeches, and internal memos. We conducted 40 hours of primary interviews, which were transcribed and analyzed for key themes. I then built a 15-chapter structure that balanced rigorous business frameworks with engaging narrative anecdotes from David’s career.

Narrative Blueprint

Pillar I: Structural Synthesis

Deciphering the 'invisible' logic that binds high-performance teams.

Pillar II: Cultural Engineering

Designing environments where innovation is the default state.
